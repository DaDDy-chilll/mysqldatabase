__Delete Record__

Delete Syntax
-DELETE FORM  tablename WHERE condition;

eg. Syntax
*will be in single
DELETE FORM customers WHERE id='xxxx';

*will be multi record
DELETE FROM customers WHERE customername='aung aung';

*Delete all records
DELETE FROM tablename;
