Select Data By Using And, Or  and Not(logical Operators)

AND OR operators are used to filter data , records based

AND - operator display a record *TRUE* 
OR - operator display a recordd *TRUE*
NOT - operator display a recordd *NOT TRUE*

WHERE clause can be combined with AND , OR , NOT operators
*real world မှာဆို တစ်ခါပဲစစ်ထုတ်ရမယ်/ အများဆုံး two 
__AND Syntax__
Method 1 AND syntax
-SELECT * FROM tablename
-WHERE country='myanmar' AND city='yangon';
 
Method 2 AND syntax
-SELECT column1,column2,...  FROM tablename
-WHERE country='myanmar' AND city='yangon';

__OR Syntax__
Method 1 OR syntax
-SELECT * FROM tablename
-WHERE NOT country='thailand';
 
__NOT Syntax__
Method 2 NOT syntax
-SELECT column1,column2,...  FROM tablename
-WHERE NOT country='thailand';
